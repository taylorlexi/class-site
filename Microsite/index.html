ThE Happiness Workshop

March 21st, 2018 - 9a.m.to 8p.m.- Sixth Avenue from 12th St. until 20th St.

The 'Happiness Workshop' is the place for you, a crazy New Yorker to come and RELAX. We can thank Mayor Bill deBlasio for setting up the event, which every New Yorker needs to attend. The event is for all New Yorkers, young and old to seperate themselves from their daily routine, go outside, and think about their own sense of being and happiness. The event is on the first day of Spring to get New Yorkers in a good mindset and excited for warmer weather. The event will have tons of activities ranging from yoga classes, to spiritual readings, to dodgeball games, to shopping games. The event is free and we hope as many people that need desbriefing from the cold, snowy winter will come!

Activities at the Street Fair

Fourty-five minute yoga classes on the streets of New York will be given all day. The instructor will vary based on the time of day you come. Yoga is an incredible way to debrief, listen to your body, and lose your mind in the inspirational sayings of your instructor or the rythmic music in the background. Matts, towels, and blocks will be supplied. Come in your stretchy yoga-wear and realx in the loudest streets in the world.

Come learn, make, and eat the delcious recipes Martha Stewart will be exhibiting at the Happiness Workshop. She will be teaching and making with us quick and easy recipes for summer: the best popsicle recipes, ice cream recipes, cookie recipes, lemon cake recipes, and how to create baskets out of watermelon. Don't miss out this once in a life time opportunity to bake with one of the best chef's in America. Baking is very therapeudic, by the end of the day you may have a new hobby that your family will love too.

There will be a variety of inspirational speakers at the Happiness workshop. People will be speaking on healthy diets, healthy minds, and their personal stories. Trip Advisor will also be showcasing different travel plans available to islands in the Bahamas as well as skii resorts in Canada. You will learn so much about others, and yourself, and may be booking a flight to the beach that night!

Have you ever found yourself wanting to talk to someome? Needing to vent but none of your friends will listen anymore? At the Happiness Workshop there will be multiple professional therapsits available to speak about anything on your mind. It's a time to let everything out and be a happier you for the new season. Everything will be between you and the therapist, and they will be available for continued sessions after the event at their specific office.

Everybody has their own activities that make them happy, but living in New York, most people worship shopping. The Happiness Workshop is going to have a couple of stores open solely for the event. After meeting the store employees at their tent, a few people at a time will be allowed to shop the entire store with a 40% discount off of everything you find. Just in time for the new summer lines!

Throwing things, hitting people, New Yorkers always want to push people out of the way. The Happiness Event will help you get all of your built up anger out with a huge 30 vs. 30 person dodgeball game. Get ready to hit, get hit, and laugh until you cry, or just cry from the intense, competitive game against other New Yorkers you've been dreaming of.
